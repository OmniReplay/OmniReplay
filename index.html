OmniReplay Application

Content creators have never had it easier when publishing immersive sports content. Viewers have never found it easier to view immersive content.

NDI® Powered Live 4K VR/360 Camera Switching

Switch between multiple live NDI® and RTMP streams in real time. Mix sources on top of one another including other live HD feeds, 3D models, text and graphics. Pair our software with an NDI® hardware solution like NewTek's Connect Spark Pro and any camera with an SDI or HDMI output and you’ve got an easy to use end to end solution for live VR/360 video. The connect spark pro has been certified to work seamlessly with our software.

Real-time 360 immersive preview

See changes such as graphics, overlays, and stats in real time both in and out of a headset. Our tools allow you to produce 360 video the way it will be consumed by viewers. No need to pre-distort graphics to fit an equirectangular video.

Live 4K Instant Replays

Create Clips for instant replays through our operator panel in real time while simultaneously recording sources to disk and streaming via RTMP to endpoints such as YouTube and Facebook. Instant replays are automatically transcoded and uploaded with XML metadata to any storage location, cloud account, CDN or social media service.

A Small Footprint

An entire multi-camera live 4K workflow can be driven off a laptop with a GTX 1060 graphics card. Leveraging NVENC by NVidia, RTMP streams can be encoded with little GPU and CPU Overhead. Additionally, NDI® with its low CPU and GPU overhead allows switching and decoding of multiple 4K sources without bogging down the live production UI.

Existing and Open Infrastructure

Content creators do not need new video infrastructure, OmniReplay can integrate with your existing production systems. Using software and hardware powered by NDI®, production teams can scale high quality real-time 4K video workflows across an existing gigabit network.

Future Proof

Works with our award-winning technologies built around the ATSC 3.0 specification. NDI® is being widely adopted throughout the industry to eliminate the need for expensive video routing and switching equipment over existing IP networks. This integration makes our software work with any supported NDI device or software.

Company

OmniReplay is a software company offering tools for live VR/360 production and instant replay workflows for immersive (AR/VR/360) sports content mixed with traditional media.

Our LiveAction 360 production software enables content creators to send instant replays in immersive formats to pair content on mobile devices with media being viewed on broadcast, cable and live streams as a value-added instant replay option.

Content providers, broadcasters and advertisers are provided with an easy to use comprehensive suite of tools to manage, integrate and produce immersive content. Content that generates high user engagement and great advertising opportunity by attracting traditional set-top viewers to the more directed and monetizable mobile experience.
